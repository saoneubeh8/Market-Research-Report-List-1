<p><p>The CCTV visual surveillance system market is categorized into three main types: Analog, Digital, and IP or Network. Analog systems utilize traditional coaxial cables and provide basic video quality, primarily suited for smaller setups. Digital systems enhance video clarity and allow for better storage and retrieval. IP or Network cameras use internet protocols to transmit high-definition video over networks, enabling remote access and advanced features such as motion detection and analytics, making them ideal for large-scale, sophisticated surveillance solutions.</p></p>
