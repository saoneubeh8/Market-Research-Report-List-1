<p><p>The miniature parts zinc die-castings market includes various types of casting techniques. Pressure die casting utilizes high pressure to inject molten zinc into molds, resulting in precise shapes. Vacuum die casting minimizes air within the mold to enhance surface finish and reduce defects. Squeeze die casting involves applying pressure to ensure dense metal flow into the mold. Semi-solid die casting uses partially solidified metal for improved mechanical properties. Other techniques may encompass unique methods catering to specific applications or design requirements.</p></p>
