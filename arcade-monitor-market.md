<p><p>The arcade monitor market consists of three main types: CRT, LCD, and LED monitors. CRT monitors, the traditional choice, offer excellent color reproduction and responsiveness but are bulkier. LCD monitors are sleek, energy-efficient, and provide good image quality, making them popular for modern arcade setups. LED monitors, an advancement of LCD technology, enhance brightness and contrast while reducing power consumption.
