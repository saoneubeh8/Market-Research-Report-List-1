<p><p>The high-temperature gaskets market encompasses various types, including metallic, semi-metallic, non-metallic, and ultra-high-temperature (UHT) liquid gaskets. Metallic gaskets, made from metals like stainless steel, offer excellent thermal resistance and durability. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal and non-metal materials, enhancing flexibility and sealing capabilities. Non-metallic gaskets, often made from materials like rubber or graphite, are suitable for lower pressures but can withstand high temperatures. UHT liquid gaskets provide high-performance sealing solutions, often used in extreme conditions for their adaptability and resilience.</p></p>
