<p><p>The cogeneration equipment market includes various technologies that efficiently produce electricity and useful heat simultaneously. Gas turbines generate power using combustion, while steam turbines utilize steam from heated water to drive generators. Electric generators convert mechanical energy into electricity. Heat Recovery Steam Generators (HRSG) capture exhaust heat from turbines for additional steam production. Boilers produce steam through combustion, and reciprocating engines convert fuel into mechanical energy. Together, these technologies optimize energy use and reduce emissions, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
