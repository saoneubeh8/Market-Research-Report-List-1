<p><p>The multimode fiber coupler market includes various configurations, primarily 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, and 2×2 couplers. A 1×2 coupler splits an input signal into two outputs, while a 1×4 coupler directs the signal to four outputs. The 1×8 type distributes the signal to eight outputs, offering broader connectivity. The 2×2 coupler, on the other hand, connects two inputs to two outputs, allowing for more flexible network designs. These couplers are essential for efficiently managing optical signals in telecommunications and data networks.</p></p>
