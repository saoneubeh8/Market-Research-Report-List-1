<p><strong>The Projection Display Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Chips</li><li>DMD Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The projection display chip market primarily consists of two types: CMOS chips and DMD chips. CMOS chips utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering advantages like low power consumption and high resolution, making them ideal for compact devices. In contrast, DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) chips employ micro-mirror technology to provide superior image quality and rapid refresh rates, predominantly used in larger projection systems. Both types cater to diverse applications, including home theaters, corporate presentations, and digital signage, shaping the evolving display landscape.</p></p>
