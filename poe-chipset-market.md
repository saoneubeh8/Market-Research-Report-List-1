<p><strong>The PoE Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Chipset</li><li>Non-Integrated Chipset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) chipset market is divided into two main types: integrated and non-integrated chipsets. Integrated chipsets combine power delivery and data transmission functionalities into a single chip, enhancing efficiency and reducing the overall size of devices. In contrast, non-integrated chipsets consist of separate components for power and data, offering flexibility in design but potentially increasing complexity and size. Each type serves various applications, from simple devices to more complex networking solutions, depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
