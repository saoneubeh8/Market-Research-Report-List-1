<p><strong>The Laminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pouch Laminator</li><li>Roll Laminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminator market primarily includes two types: pouch laminators and roll laminators. Pouch laminators utilize pre-cut laminate pouches to seal documents, making them ideal for smaller tasks and home use. In contrast, roll laminators use large rolls of laminate film, allowing for continuous feeding of materials, which is suitable for high-volume applications in professional settings such as print shops and schools. Both types enhance document durability and protect against wear, moisture, and fading.</p></p>
