<p><ul><li>Single Station Tablet Presses</li><li>Multi Station Tablet Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tablet presses market is primarily divided into two types: single station tablet presses and multi-station tablet presses. Single station tablet presses, also known as single punch presses, operate one tablet at a time, ideal for small-scale production and research purposes. In contrast, multi-station tablet presses, or rotary presses, can produce multiple tablets simultaneously, making them suitable for large-scale manufacturing due to their higher efficiency and output. Both types cater to varying production needs within the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
