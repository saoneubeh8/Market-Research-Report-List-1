<p><strong>The Pot Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Sliding Pot Bearing</li><li>Fixed Pot Bearing</li><li>Guided Pot Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pot bearing market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Free Sliding Pot Bearings** allow longitudinal movement and are designed to accommodate horizontal forces, making them suitable for bridges and structures requiring flexibility.</p><p>2. **Fixed Pot Bearings** restrict movements to vertical directions, providing stability for structures where lateral movement is not desired.</p><p>3. **Guided Pot Bearings** combine features of both, allowing controlled movement while preventing excessive lateral displacement. Each type is crucial for maintaining structural integrity and functionality in engineering applications.</p></p>
