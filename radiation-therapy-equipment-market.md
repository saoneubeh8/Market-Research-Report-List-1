<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiation Therapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiation Therapy</li><li>Internal Radiation Therapy</li><li>Systemic Radiation Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation therapy equipment encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT)** uses high-energy beams from outside the body to target tumors, employing machines like linear accelerators. </p><p>2. **Internal Radiation Therapy** (brachytherapy) involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, allowing for localized treatment. </p><p>3. **Systemic Radiation Therapy** administers radioactive substances through the bloodstream, targeting cancer cells throughout the body, ideal for specific cancers like thyroid malignancies. Each type offers distinct benefits based on cancer type and treatment goals.</p></p>
