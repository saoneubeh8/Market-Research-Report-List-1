<p><p>The textile anthraquinone dyes market is divided into two main types: soluble and insoluble dyes. Soluble dyes easily dissolve in water and are used for direct applications on fabrics, offering vibrant colors and versatility. In contrast, insoluble dyes remain solid and require fixatives or specific processes for adherence to textiles. Each type caters to distinct application needs, with soluble dyes often favored for high-quality, bright finishes, while insoluble dyes are preferred for their durability and resistance to fading.</p></p>
