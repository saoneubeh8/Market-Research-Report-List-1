<p><ul><li>Coreless Inner Rotor Type</li><li>Core Inner Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DD Direct Drive Motor market features two main types: Coreless Inner Rotor Type and Core Inner Rotor Type. The Coreless Inner Rotor Type boasts a lightweight design and high efficiency due to the absence of a core, allowing for smooth operation and enhanced responsiveness. In contrast, the Core Inner Rotor Type incorporates a solid core, providing greater torque and stability but typically resulting in a heavier configuration. Both types are used in applications requiring precise control and high performance, such as robotics and automation.</p></p>
