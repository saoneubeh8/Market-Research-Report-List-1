<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Encoder</li><li>Magnetic Encoder</li><li>Capacitive and Inductive Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angle encoder market comprises various types, including photoelectric, magnetic, capacitive, and inductive encoders. Photoelectric encoders use light to detect angle changes, offering high precision. Magnetic encoders utilize magnetic fields for reliable position sensing, making them robust in harsh environments. Capacitive encoders measure changes in capacitance for angular measurements, ideal for applications requiring compact designs. Inductive encoders operate based on electromagnetic induction, providing high resolution and durability. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, influencing market demand and innovation.</p></p>
