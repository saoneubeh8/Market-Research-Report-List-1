<p><strong>The Fan Coil Unit (FCU) Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Pipe FCU Valve</li><li>Four Pipe FCU Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fan Coil Unit (FCU) Valve market is mainly segmented into two types: Two Pipe FCU Valves and Four Pipe FCU Valves. Two Pipe FCU Valves utilize a single set of pipes for both heating and cooling, making them more cost-effective and simpler for smaller systems. In contrast, Four Pipe FCU Valves operate with separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing for greater flexibility and simultaneous temperature control in different zones, catering to larger and more complex HVAC systems.</p></p>
