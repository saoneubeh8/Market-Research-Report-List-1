<p><p>The aluminum alloy market is segmented into heat-treatable and non-heat-treatable alloys. Heat-treatable alloys include the 2xxx series (copper-based, high strength, used in aerospace), 6xxx series (magnesium-silicon, good corrosion resistance, often found in structural applications), and 7xxx series (zinc-based, known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, ideal for aerospace and sports equipment). Non-heat-treatable alloys comprise the 3xxx series (manganese-based, excellent corrosion resistance for beverage cans), 4xxx series (silicon-based, common in welding), and 5xxx series (magnesium-based, good for marine environments). Other market segments include specific niche applications and specialty alloys.</p></p>
