<p><p>The Laser Vibrometer Sensor market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Laser Doppler Vibrometer Sensor**: Utilizes the Doppler effect to measure vibration by detecting frequency changes in reflected laser light.</p><p>2. **Interferometric Laser Vibrometer Sensor**: Employs interference patterns from two laser beams to achieve high precision in vibration measurement, ideal for fine structural analysis.</p><p>3. **Photoelectric Laser Vibrometer Sensor**: Combines laser technology with photoelectric sensors for real-time vibration monitoring, offering versatility in various applications. </p><p>Each type serves distinct industry needs based on accuracy and measurement range.</p></p>
