<p><p>The Injection Site Adapters market includes three main types: Needleless Connectors, Heparin Caps, and Tee Joints. Needleless Connectors facilitate safe medication administration without using needles, reducing the risk of needle-stick injuries. Heparin Caps are designed to maintain catheter patency and prevent clot formation, providing a sterile closed system. Tee Joints serve as multi-port access points, allowing for simultaneous infusions and drug delivery, enhancing efficiency in patient care. Together, these adapters improve safety and streamline intravenous therapies.</p></p>
