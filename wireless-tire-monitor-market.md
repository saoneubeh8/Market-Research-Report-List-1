<p><strong>The Wireless Tire Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built-in</li><li>External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Tire Monitor Market can be divided into two main types: built-in and external. Built-in systems are integrated directly into the vehicle, providing real-time tire pressure and temperature data through the existing onboard computer. This offers seamless monitoring without additional components. External systems, on the other hand, consist of individual sensors attached to the tire valves, transmitting data to a separate display or app. They are easier to install and can be retrofitted to various vehicles, providing flexibility for users.</p></p>
