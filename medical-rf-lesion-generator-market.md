<p><strong>The Medical RF Lesion Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Radio Frequency (RF)</li><li>Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical RF Lesion Generator market includes Conventional Radio Frequency (RF) and Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF) technologies. Conventional RF generates continuous high-frequency currents to create thermal lesions in tissues, effectively blocking pain signals. In contrast, PRF utilizes intermittent pulses of energy, producing less heat and sparing surrounding tissues while still effectively modulating pain. Both technologies are utilized for various pain management and therapeutic procedures, catering to different clinical needs and patient conditions while driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
