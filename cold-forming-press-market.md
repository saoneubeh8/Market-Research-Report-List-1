<p><p>The cold forming press market is segmented into two primary types: electric and hydraulic. Conversely, hydraulic cold forming presses use hydraulic systems to deliver high force and are often favored for their ability to handle complex shapes and larger production volumes. Both types serve distinct applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, catering to varying production needs.</p></p>
