<p><strong>The Rotary Lime Kiln Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fire</li><li>Indirect Fire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary lime kiln market consists of two primary types: direct fire and indirect fire kilns. Direct fire kilns involve the combustion of fuel directly within the kiln, generating the necessary heat to produce lime, making them efficient for high-capacity production. In contrast, indirect fire kilns use a heat exchanger system where heat is transferred without contact with the lime, offering better temperature control and reduced emissions. Each type serves specific industrial applications, catering to varying efficiency and environmental standards.</p></p>
