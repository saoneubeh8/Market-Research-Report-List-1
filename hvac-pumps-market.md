<p><strong>The HVAC Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC pumps market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps feature a single impeller, making them ideal for low-pressure applications, delivering efficient water flow in residential and light commercial systems. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressures, suitable for applications requiring significant elevation, such as large buildings or industrial processes. Each type serves specific requirements, making them essential for effective heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
