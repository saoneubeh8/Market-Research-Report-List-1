<p><p>The Operation Theatre Light market is primarily divided into two types: Halogen lights and Light Emitting Diodes (LED). Halogen lights provide bright, focused illumination and are known for their color accuracy. However, they generate more heat and consume more energy. In contrast, LED lights are rapidly gaining popularity due to their energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower heat output. LEDs also offer adjustable brightness and color temperature, enhancing visibility during surgeries, making them a preferred choice in modern operating rooms.</p></p>
