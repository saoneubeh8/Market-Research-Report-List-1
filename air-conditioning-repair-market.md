<p><ul><li>Whole-Home</li><li>Split Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning repair market can be categorized primarily into whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems are designed to cool entire residences, utilizing ductwork for even distribution of air. Repair services for these units often involve addressing complex issues related to ductwork and central units. Conversely, split systems consist of an outdoor condenser and indoor air handlers, offering more flexibility and zoning options. Repair for split systems typically focuses on individual components, making it essential for targeted troubleshooting.</p></p>
