<p><p>The pedicle screws market consists of three primary types: polyaxial, monoaxial, and cannulated screws. Polyaxial screws offer multi-directional angling, allowing for flexible placement in the vertebrae, ideal for complex spinal surgeries. Monoaxial screws have a single axis of movement, providing stability in straightforward applications. Cannulated screws feature a hollow design that enables guided placement over a wire, facilitating minimally invasive procedures. Each type serves distinct surgical needs and enhances spinal fixation outcomes in orthopedic and neurosurgical applications.</p></p>
