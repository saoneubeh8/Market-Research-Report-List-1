<p><strong>The High Performance Inertial Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angular Rate Gyro</li><li>Line Accelerometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance inertial sensors market encompasses various technologies, primarily Angular Rate Gyros (ARG) and Linear Accelerometers (LA). Angular Rate Gyros measure rotational motion, providing precise angular velocity data crucial for navigation and stabilization in aerospace, automotive, and robotics applications. Linear Accelerometers, on the other hand, gauge linear motion, detecting changes in velocity along a specific axis. Together, these sensors enable advanced motion tracking and orientation control, driving advancements in autonomous systems, wearable technology, and augmented reality applications.</p></p>
