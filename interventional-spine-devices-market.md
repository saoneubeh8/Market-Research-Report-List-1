<p><p>The interventional spine devices market includes various techniques for treating spinal conditions. Vertebroplasty involves injecting cement into fractured vertebrae to provide stability and pain relief. Kyphoplasty is similar but includes creating space before cement injection, often restoring vertebra height. Radiofrequency ablation utilizes heat generated by radio waves to target and disrupt nerve signals responsible for pain. These minimally invasive procedures aim to enhance patient outcomes, reduce recovery times, and improve quality of life for those with spine-related conditions.</p></p>
