<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Type</li><li>Analog Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Differential pressure transmitters measure pressure differences between two points, crucial in various industries. The market consists of two main types: digital and analog. Digital differential pressure transmitters utilize microprocessor technology to provide precise measurements and advanced features, such as data logging and remote monitoring, making them suitable for complex applications. Analog types, in contrast, offer simpler, real-time readings using mechanical components and are often favored for straightforward installations where high precision is not critical. Both serve essential roles in process control and monitoring.</p></p>
