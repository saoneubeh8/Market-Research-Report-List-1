<p><ul><li>Multi-stage</li><li>Single-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submersible 3-phase electric water pumps market is primarily classified into two types: multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps feature multiple impellers arranged in series, allowing for higher pressure and suitable for deep well applications. They are ideal for transporting water over long distances. In contrast, single-stage pumps have one impeller and are designed for lower pressure applications, making them suitable for relatively shallow water sources. Each type serves specific needs based on depth and pressure requirements.</p></p>
