<p><p>The Industrial Computed Tomography Equipment market comprises two main types: stationary and portable equipment. Stationary computed tomography equipment is fixed in a specific location, offering high-resolution imaging and is typically used for large-scale inspections in manufacturing facilities. In contrast, portable computed tomography equipment is mobile and can be transported to various sites, providing flexibility for on-the-go inspections, especially in remote or challenging environments. Both types play crucial roles in quality control, materials analysis, and non-destructive testing in various industries.</p></p>
