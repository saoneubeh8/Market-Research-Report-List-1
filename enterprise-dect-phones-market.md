<p><strong>The Enterprise DECT Phones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SIP DECT</li><li>IP DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enterprise DECT Phones market comprises two main types: SIP DECT and IP DECT. SIP DECT integrates Session Initiation Protocol technology, allowing seamless communication over IP networks and enabling interoperability with various VoIP systems. IP DECT, on the other hand, focuses on leveraging Internet Protocol for direct communication between handsets and multi-cell base stations, enhancing coverage and mobility in larger enterprises. Both types are essential for businesses seeking efficient wireless communication solutions to support their operations.</p></p>
