<p><strong>The Handgun Red Dot Sights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SFP</li><li>FFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the handgun red dot sight market, two primary types are prevalent: Second Focal Plane (SFP) and First Focal Plane (FFP). SFP sights maintain a consistent reticle size regardless of magnification, ensuring ease of target acquisition at various distances, making them popular for close-range shooting. In contrast, FFP sights adjust the reticle size with magnification changes, allowing for more accurate holdovers at different ranges, appealing to precision shooters. Each type caters to specific user preferences and shooting needs.</p></p>
