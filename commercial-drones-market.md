<p><p>The commercial drones market is primarily segmented into three types: fixed-wing drones, rotary blade drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones are designed for long-range flights and are efficient for large area surveys. Rotary blade drones, or multirotors, offer greater maneuverability and are ideal for short-range tasks like aerial photography and inspections. Hybrid drones combine features of both, providing the endurance of fixed-wings with the versatility of rotary systems, making them suitable for diverse applications in industries like agriculture, logistics, and surveillance.</p></p>
