<p><p>Pharmaceutical cold chain logistics encompasses various transportation modes to ensure the safe delivery of temperature-sensitive medications. Sea freight is cost-effective for large shipments but may require longer transit times, risking temperature control. Airfreight offers speed and reliable temperature management, ideal for urgent deliveries. Overland transport involves trucks equipped with temperature-controlled systems, commonly used for regional distribution. The integrated market combines these methods, leveraging intermodal solutions to optimize efficiency and maintain strict temperature compliance throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
