<p><p>The residential air conditioners market consists of several key components that work together to provide cooling. The compressor circulates refrigerant and enhances pressure, while the condenser dissipates heat outside. The metering device regulates refrigerant flow into the cooling coil, which absorbs heat from the indoor air. The air handling unit distributes cooled air throughout the space. Finally, the duct system ensures efficient airflow, connecting various rooms and maintaining a comfortable indoor environment. Each component plays a crucial role in overall efficiency and performance.</p></p>
