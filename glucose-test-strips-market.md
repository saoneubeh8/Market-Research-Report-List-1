<p><p>The glucose test strips market primarily comprises three types: Glucose Oxidase Test Strips, Glucose Dehydrogenase Test Strips, and Other test strips. Glucose Oxidase strips utilize the enzyme glucose oxidase to oxidize glucose, producing hydrogen peroxide for measurement. Glucose Dehydrogenase strips rely on glucose dehydrogenase, generating an electrical signal proportional to glucose levels. Other types may include various technologies or enzymes, catering to specific user needs or preferences, thus expanding options for blood glucose monitoring in different patient populations.</p></p>
