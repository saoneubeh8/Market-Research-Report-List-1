<p><p>Slaughter systems can be categorized into stationary and mobile systems. Stationary slaughtering equipment is permanently set up at a facility, offering efficiency for high-volume processing. It typically includes assembly lines with specialized machinery for various stages of slaughtering, ensuring consistent output and regulatory compliance. In contrast, mobile slaughter vehicles provide on-site processing, catering to smaller farms or specific locations. These units promote animal welfare by reducing transport stress and can adapt to varying scales of operation, enhancing local supply chains.</p></p>
