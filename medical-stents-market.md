<p><p>Medical stents are tiny tubes inserted into narrowed or blocked blood vessels or other hollow organs to restore normal fluid flow. They are commonly used in procedures such as angioplasty to treat coronary artery disease. Stents can be made from various materials, including metal and biodegradable substances. Their primary purpose is to prevent the collapse of the vessel, maintain blood flow, and support the structure of the organ involved.</p><p>The Medical Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in stent technology, and a growing aging population. Key trends include the rising adoption of drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent arterial blockage, and the development of bioresorbable stents that dissolve over time, minimizing long-term complications. Additionally, increasing healthcare expenditure and the expansion of research and development initiatives in the field of interventional cardiology are anticipated to further fuel market growth. As the demand for minimally invasive procedures rises, the Medical Stents Market is poised for significant developments in both product innovation and market opportunities.</p></p>
