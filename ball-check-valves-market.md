<p><p>Ball check valves are non-return valves that utilize a spherical ball to prevent backflow in piping systems. When fluid flows in the intended direction, the ball is lifted off its seat, allowing flow through the valve. Conversely, when backflow occurs, the ball is pushed back onto the seat, effectively blocking the reverse flow. These valves are widely used in various industries, including oil and gas, water and wastewater treatment, and power generation, due to their reliability and efficiency in preventing backflow.</p><p>The Ball Check Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period. <p><p>The ball check valves market is categorized into two main types based on connection methods: flanged connection and threaded connection. Flanged connection valves feature flat surfaces with bolt holes, enabling easy installation and disconnection, ideal for larger pipelines. In contrast, threaded connection valves screw directly onto pipes, offering compact design and simpler assembly, suitable for smaller systems. These types cater to varying application needs, ensuring efficient fluid flow management while preventing backflow in diverse industrial settings.</p></p>
