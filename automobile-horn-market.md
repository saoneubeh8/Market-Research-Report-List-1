<p><p>The automobile horn market is primarily divided into two types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud, attention-grabbing sound, commonly found in trucks and emergency vehicles due to their powerful output. In contrast, electric horns operate on electrical systems, generating sound through vibrating diaphragms. They are typically used in cars and motorcycles for everyday signaling, offering a range of tones. Both types serve essential safety functions, enhancing vehicle visibility and communication on the road.</p></p>
