<p><p>The rice noodles market consists of three main types: fresh, dried, and instant rice noodles. Fresh rice noodles are typically made from uncooked rice flour and have a soft texture, often used in dishes like stir-fries and soups. Dried rice noodles have a longer shelf life and require soaking or boiling before use, making them versatile for various cuisines. Instant rice noodles are precooked and dehydrated, allowing for quick preparation, ideal for convenience meals and snacks. Each type caters to different consumer needs and culinary applications.</p></p>
