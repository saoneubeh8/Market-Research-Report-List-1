<p><p>Visual sensor systems can be categorized into three types based on dimensionality. 1D systems process linear data, such as simple light intensity or color measurements along a single axis, often used in applications like barcode scanners. 2D systems capture flat images, enabling applications such as cameras and facial recognition, providing more detailed spatial information. 3D systems offer depth perception, generating three-dimensional models, which are crucial for autonomous vehicles and robotics, enhancing navigation and interaction in complex environments.</p></p>
